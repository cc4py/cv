Charles A. Chang, PhD

Biotech Scientist
Islet Cell & BMT Specialist
San Francisco Bay Area, California

I am a biomedical scientist and cell therapy manufacturing consultant currently working in the SF Bay Area.
My research experience has spanned from organic chemistry synthesis of vaccine adjuvants and antibody-drug conjugates, to chemical modification and reducing inflammation of transplanted living tissue, and immune tolerance and treatment of autoimmune disease through bone marrow chimerism.

My professional goals are to use my strong technical skills and experience to advance curative treatments in the cell therapy and biologics spaces.
